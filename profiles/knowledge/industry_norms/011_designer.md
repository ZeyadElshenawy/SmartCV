# Product / UX / UI Designer — Resume Conventions

Designer resumes are evaluated against a different bar than engineering resumes. The artifact (the portfolio) is heavily weighted; the resume mainly serves to (a) list verifiable employment, (b) summarize tools and craft, and (c) name the few highest-impact projects so the recruiter knows what to look for in the portfolio.

**Tech-stack categories:**

- **Design tools:** Figma (dominant; advanced use — variables, components, Dev Mode, FigJam), Sketch (legacy), Adobe XD.
- **Prototyping:** Figma, Framer, ProtoPie, Principle, Lottie.
- **Visual / motion:** Adobe Creative Suite, Rive, Spline.
- **Research:** Maze, UserTesting, Lookback, Dovetail.
- **Analytics:** Hotjar, FullStory, Amplitude, Mixpanel, Pendo.
- **Design systems:** Storybook, Zeroheight, Supernova.

**Metrics:**
- **Launched-design outcomes:** conversion lift, task-completion rate, retention shift, NPS delta, ticket-volume reduction.
- **Research scope:** # interviews, # usability sessions, # survey responses.
- **Process:** design-iteration count, brief-to-spec time, review-cycle reduction.
- **Adoption:** # surfaces, # components in system, # teams consuming.
- **Accessibility:** WCAG level, axe-violation count, screen-reader coverage.

**Strong bullet examples for designer:**

- "Redesigned the checkout flow from 5 screens to 2 after analyzing 220 funnel drop-offs in Hotjar; A/B test on 8K users (Optimizely, p<0.01) showed cart-abandonment fell from 38% to 24%."
- "Shipped v2 of the design system (Figma, 84 components, 12 token primitives, WCAG AA); adopted across 6 surfaces, replacing 18 ad-hoc styles."
- "Led 14 user interviews + 22 usability sessions for the onboarding redesign; persona map informed navigation refactor that lifted day-1 activation from 31% to 40%."
- "Reduced design-review cycles from typical 4 to 1.5 across Q3 via a design-spec template + Figma component-coverage checklist at the handoff gate."
- "Co-designed the mobile accessibility audit fixes (with one engineer, 6 weeks); brought iOS app from 38 axe violations to 0 across 14 core flows; rating moved from 4.1 to 4.5."

**Designer-specific anti-patterns:**

- "Designed beautiful interfaces" — subjective. Replace with testable artifact or measured outcome.
- Tool-only bullets: a tag list, not an accomplishment.
- No metrics. Designers often have access to product analytics; recruiters expect 2–3 outcome bullets.
- Listing every portfolio project. List 3–5 highest-impact only.

**Portfolio link required.** Designer resumes without a portfolio link are widely viewed as incomplete by recruiters for this role family. Portfolio should load <3s, show 4–8 case studies, include research artifacts for at least 2, have one shipped-to-production case with an outcome metric.

**IC vs. design-lead distinction:**
- **IC:** craft work, individual ownership, research execution.
- **Design lead:** team mentorship, design-system governance, cross-functional alignment, hiring.

Surface which mode dominates in the Summary.

## Concrete rule for SmartCV

For designer roles, surface tools across Design, Prototyping, Research, Analytics, and Design Systems sub-groups. Generate 3–5 bullets per role; each must include either a measured outcome (conversion lift, task-completion %, NPS delta), a research scope (N interviews, N usability sessions), or an adoption count (N surfaces, N components, N teams). Always include a portfolio link in contact info. Distinguish IC craft work from design-lead / manager work in the Professional Summary.
